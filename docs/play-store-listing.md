## Full Description

Your memories deserve more than a camera roll. The Memory Palace is a 3D memory keeper and digital time capsule where your photos, videos, and life stories come alive in a beautiful interactive space.

BUILD YOUR PERSONAL MEMORY PALACE
Walk through a stunning 3D palace you design yourself. Create wings for different chapters of your life — childhood, travel, family, career — and fill rooms with the photos, videos, and stories that define your journey.

PRESERVE YOUR FAMILY LEGACY
Upload photos and videos, write life stories, and record voice interviews with loved ones using guided questions. Create a digital legacy that future generations can explore and cherish.

SHARE MEMORIES WITH FAMILY
Invite family members to contribute their own memories and stories. Build a shared family history together — like a private family photo album that grows across generations.

SMART MEMORY ORGANIZATION
AI-powered tagging automatically organizes your memories. Import from Google Photos, Dropbox, OneDrive, or Box. Create beautiful public gallery links or QR codes to share specific memories.

KEY FEATURES
- 3D interactive memory palace you can explore
- Photo album, video, and story uploads
- AI memory tagging and smart organization
- Voice recording interviews with guided prompts
- Family sharing and collaboration tools
- Digital time capsule and legacy planning
- Cloud import from Google Photos, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box
- Public sharing via gallery links and QR codes
- Full data export anytime
- GDPR compliant with EU-hosted encrypted storage

PRIVACY AND SECURITY
Your memories are private by default. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, hosted on EU infrastructure, and fully GDPR compliant. You own your data — export or delete everything at any time.

Start free with 1 wing, 3 rooms, and 50 memories. Upgrade for more space and premium features like AI organization and family sharing.
